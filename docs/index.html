WORLD PEACE, PROSPERITY, AND JOY

The world amazing framework, plan, and proposed institution is designed to unify global resources, services, and populations under one integrated system to promote efficiency and cooperation.

By fostering cooperation between all nations, cultures, and peoples, the framework will harness the collective potential of humanity to create a truly incredible, comfortable, safe, and interconnected world.

The framework aims to replace traditional governments, NGOs, and separate international entities by providing all essential services—including education, housing, and healthcare—through collective efforts and advanced, sustainable technologies, ensuring that every individual can live comfortably by contributing on average just two days of work per week and allowing everyone to retire from demanding physical or mental labor by age 50, at the latest.

The global framework champions creativity, empowering every person to discover and enhance their talents, fostering an environment where innovation and artistic expression flourish.

Every individual will have the opportunity to excel in all areas of human capability, including intellectual, emotional, physical, and creative, with everyone able to sing, dance, and play instruments at the highest levels, reflecting the full expression of these capacities.

Embracing inclusivity, the framework ensures that all, including those with troubled pasts or in prison, regardless of age, ability, background, or location, are actively included, provided with resources to grow, and valued, guaranteeing that no one is forgotten and every life is empowered.
